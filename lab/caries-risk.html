LAB / CARIES RISK

Six questions, one risk tier.

A working estimate of how the next twelve months are likely to go for your teeth. The point is not to scare you; the point is to know which two habits matter most for you specifically.

01 / SUGAR FREQUENCY

How often do you have something sweet or sugary (drinks, snacks, fruit juice)?

02 / BRUSHING

How often do you brush thoroughly (full 2 minutes, fluoride toothpaste)?

03 / FLUORIDE EXPOSURE

Beyond brushing, do you get fluoride from drinking water or mouthwash?

04 / DRY MOUTH

Do you experience dry mouth (medications, mouth-breathing at night, dehydration)?

05 / PRIOR CARIES

Have you had a new cavity diagnosed in the last 3 years?

06 / LAST DENTAL VISIT

When was your last dental check?
