LAB / PLAQUE SCORE

A five question self check.

Honest answers in two minutes. The score is a rough plaque-control signal, not a diagnosis. Use it to decide whether you should be cleaning more thoughtfully, or booking the cleaning you have been putting off.

01 / BLEEDING

Do your gums bleed when you brush or floss?

02 / VISIBLE PLAQUE

After brushing, can you still see or feel a film on the gum line?

03 / TONGUE

Is your tongue coated when you wake up?

04 / BREATH

Has anyone close to you mentioned your breath, or do you notice it yourself?

05 / LAST CLEANING

When was your last professional dental cleaning?
