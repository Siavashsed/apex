When teeth show up.

A reference for parents and curious adults. Use the typical windows for orientation, not as a deadline. Variation of several months in either direction is normal and rarely cause for concern.

A useful rule: cleaning starts the day the first tooth appears. Wipe with a damp cloth, then a soft brush as more arrive. Stay away from sugary drinks in a bottle at bedtime.
