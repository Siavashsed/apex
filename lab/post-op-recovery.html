LAB / POST-OP RECOVERY

After a tooth extraction.

A four-phase checklist for the first two weeks. Tick as you go - progress is saved locally to this device. Honour the order. The first 24 hours protect the clot; everything else builds on that.

RED FLAGS - SEEK CARE

Call your dentist or go to urgent care if

  • Heavy bleeding does not slow after 90 minutes of firm gauze pressure.
  • Severe pain that worsens after day 3 (a sign of dry socket).
  • Swelling that grows after day 3, or you cannot open your mouth, or you develop a fever above 38°C.
  • Numbness in the lip, tongue, or chin that persists past 48 hours.
  • A foul taste or smell that does not resolve with saltwater rinses after day 3.
This tool does not replace clinical advice.
